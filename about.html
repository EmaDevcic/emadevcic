Hi there, I'm Ema!

From an early age, it was evident that my future involved being a designer. My deep interest in art, fantasy, movies, and books fueled my passion for the creative arts. When it came time to choose a career, design was the only path that truly resonated with me.

As I delved deeper into the field, I recognized the paramount importance of user-centred design, which led me to specialize in UX. Today, I am a dedicated designer committed to delivering exceptional digital products through empathizing with users.

This website and the work showcased here are a testament to my profound passion for design and my strong work ethic. I am confident that these qualities will significantly benefit me as a UX designer in this ever-evolving industry.
