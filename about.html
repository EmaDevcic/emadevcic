Commitment

From a young age, it was clear my future involved being a designer. I painted castles on my bedroom wall, carried a camera with me everywhere I went, made my own clothes (or at least tried too). My deep interest in drawing, fantasy, movies, and books fuelled my passion for the creative arts. So, when the time came to choose a career, there was only one option that truly spoke to me.

The more I learned about design, the more I realized how important user-centred design is. Realisation which led me to the field of UX. Today I am a dedicated designer committed to deliver exceptional digital products thought empathising with users.

This website and the showcased work are a testament to my immense passion for design and strong work ethic. I firmly believe that these qualities will greatly benefit me as a UX designer in an ever-evolving industry.
