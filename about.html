Hi there, I'm Ema!

My journey into the world of design started with a fascination for art, fantasy, movies, and books, which naturally led me to pursue a career in design. Early on, I immersed myself in web design, captivated by the process of bringing ideas to life online. However, as I gained more experience, I realized that the true power of design lies not just in aesthetics but in creating meaningful experiences for users.

This discovery led me to the field of UX, where I found my true passion designing with the user at the heart of every decision. Today, I am committed to crafting digital products that not only look beautiful but also solve real problems and resonate with users on a deeper level.

This website, along with the projects showcased here, reflects my dedication to thoughtful, user-centered design. I'm excited to continue evolving in this dynamic industry and bring my skills and passion for UX to every project I undertake.
