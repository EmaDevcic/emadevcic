Ohvaz

Role UX Designer
Tools Figma
Part of Udemy Figma Certificate

One of my first design projects in Figma was the Ohvaz platform, which I developed as part of the Udemy course "Figma for Web Design: Master UI/UX in Figma." This project allowed me to gain valuable skills in Figma, such as designing user interfaces, creating and manipulating vector graphics, components, and developing interactive prototypes with transitions and animations. I also learned how to

efficiently organize and manage design assets, collaborate with team members on design projects, and prepare designs for clients and web developers. The final task was to create an interactive digital prototype for an e-commerce website called Ohvaz, ensuring that the desktop version could easily be adapted for small devices while maintaining a clear and simple UI with only the most important information presented.
