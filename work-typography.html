Creative Typography

Role Graphic Designer
Tools Adobe Illustrator
Part of Multimedia Undegraduate Degree

This project was about creating a dynamic design using typography. My work shows typography letters emerging from a book and creating an image of a sailing ship, specifically Drakkar - Viking ship. This project took a lot of time and

patience, as the position of every letter is important. For example, the sail of a ship is formatted out of letters for the word sail. The idea behind the design is that while we are reading, words create images in our minds.
